Timeline

The Atomic Journey

🏐
1803

John Dalton

Model: Solid, indivisible sphere - like billiard balls!

Discovery: Each element has its own type of atom.

Limitation: Didn't know about subatomic particles!

🍮
1897

J.J. Thomson

Model: Plum pudding - positive sphere with embedded electrons!

Discovery: Discovered the electron using cathode ray tubes.

Limitation: No nucleus - couldn't explain gold foil experiment!

🎯
1911

Ernest Rutherford

Model: Dense nucleus with electrons orbiting - mostly empty space!

Discovery: Gold foil experiment revealed the nucleus.

Limitation: Electrons should spiral into nucleus!

🪐
1913

Niels Bohr

Model: Electrons in fixed energy levels - like planets!

Discovery: Quantized energy levels explain spectral lines.

Limitation: Only works for hydrogen atom!
